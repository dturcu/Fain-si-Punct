# Fain si Punct Brand Voice

## Tone
- Professional but approachable. Not corporate, not casual.
- Confident without being pushy. We inform, we don't pressure.
- Romanian language — natural, not translated from English.
- Use diacritics in content where appropriate (blog, product descriptions).

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Pret corect** — "Cele mai bune preturi" is our core promise
2. **Incredere** — Retur in 30 zile, plata la livrare, livrare gratuita
3. **Varietate** — Peste 14.000 produse in catalog
4. **Simplicitate** — Comanda rapida, fara bataie de cap

## Voice Examples
- Good: "Comanda acum si primesti livrare gratuita"
- Bad: "GRABESTE-TE! Oferta LIMITATA!!!"
- Good: "Produsul este disponibil si poate fi livrat in 2-3 zile"
- Bad: "Nu rata aceasta oportunitate UNICA"

## Product Descriptions
- Lead with the benefit, not the feature
- Include dimensions, material, and use case where relevant
- No filler phrases ("produs de calitate superioara")
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences max)
