# Fain si Punct Internal Links Map

## Primary Pages (highest authority)
- `/` — Homepage (hub page, links to all categories)
- `/products` — Product catalog (filterable, sortable)
- `/products/[id]` — Product detail pages (~14k pages)

## Category Hubs
- `/products?category=[name]` — Category filtered views
- These should cross-link to related categories

## Trust / Conversion Pages
- `/about` — About us (link from footer, product pages)
- `/contact` — Contact (link from footer, FAQ)
- `/faq` — FAQ (link from product pages, checkout)
- `/returns` — Return policy (link from product pages, checkout, cart)

## Legal Pages
- `/terms` — Terms and conditions
- `/privacy` — Privacy policy
- `/cookies` — Cookie policy

## Cross-linking Strategy
1. Product pages → related products (already implemented)
2. Product pages → category page via breadcrumbs (already implemented)
3. Category pages → related categories (to implement)
4. Blog posts → product pages (when blog is built)
5. FAQ → relevant product categories
